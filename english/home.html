Welcome to Our School!

Do you know that we have the strongest school curriculum that is exclusively based on the guidelines of the ‘National Education Policy (NEP)’ 2020? There are many factors that make us one of the most unique schools. These factors are: Smart Classes, Virtual Reality (VR), Teaching Learning Material, Science & Technology, Project Work and extra activities with bhajans and patriotic songs which focus on promoting the overall development of your child. We provide your child with a home like environment where their mind is nurtured in the best possible manner. Our preschool curriculum is designed by a team of experienced teachers to ensure that children are introduced to essential skills and concepts in a fun and enjoyable way.

Our Teaching Methodology!

We have developed this method through our 12 years of hard work, in-depth study of child psychology and our experiences. On this basis, we have divided the teaching methods into three groups:

The aim of Group-1 is to help children learn language, behaviour and the art of living among friends so that they can be successful in their lives.

Pre Nur - Nur

The aim of Group-2 is to develop children's academic, social and thinking abilities and to ensure that your child is fully prepared to move to the primary group.

LKG - UKG

The aim of Group-3 is to develop a desire for lifelong learning and promote intellectual, emotional, and social growth in a supportive environment.
